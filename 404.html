❓ FAQ

Everything you need
to know before starting

Is Printify really free to use? +
Yes — Printify's free plan lets you create unlimited products, connect 3 stores, and start selling with no monthly fee. You only pay the base product cost when a customer places an order. There's also a Printify Premium plan ($29/month) that gives you up to 20% off all products, which pays for itself quickly at scale.
How much money can I make with Printify? +
Your profit is the difference between your retail price and Printify's base cost. For example, a t-shirt might cost $12 from Printify — you sell it for $29, keeping $17 profit. Many sellers make $500–$5,000+ per month part-time. Full-time sellers routinely earn $10,000–$50,000+ monthly. Your income scales with your effort and marketing.
Do I need design skills to use Printify? +
Not at all! Printify's Product Creator is built for everyone. You can upload simple text-based designs, use free tools like Canva to create graphics, or hire affordable designers from Fiverr. Many top sellers use simple text quotes on t-shirts and make thousands per month.
How does shipping and fulfillment work? +
When a customer places an order in your store, Printify automatically receives it, sends it to the nearest print provider, and ships it directly to your customer — usually within 2–7 business days. You never see, touch, or store any inventory. Everything is 100% hands-free.
What platforms can I sell on with Printify? +
Printify integrates natively with Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, Wix, Squarespace, eBay, TikTok Shop, and more. You can also use Printify's own Pop-Up Store feature to start selling immediately without any other platform. Many sellers start with Etsy (free listings) or Shopify.
Is Printify available outside the US? +
Absolutely. Printify is a truly global platform — you can sell from any country and ship to 100+ countries worldwide. Print providers are located in the US, UK, Europe, Canada, and Australia, enabling fast local delivery across major markets. Payouts are supported in multiple currencies.
How is Printify different from Printful? +
The biggest difference is that Printify has 90+ print providers to choose from, meaning you get competitive pricing and can switch providers for better quality or faster shipping. Printify also generally offers lower base costs than Printful, which means higher profit margins for you. Both are excellent — but Printify wins on price and product variety.
